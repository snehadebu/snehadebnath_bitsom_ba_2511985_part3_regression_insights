# Final Recommendation

## Executive Summary

The objective was to identify factors associated with monthly sales performance.

## Key Findings

1. Footfall is the strongest predictor of sales.

2. Marketing spend has a positive but weaker relationship.

3. Inventory availability likely supports higher sales performance.

4. Regional differences may influence outcomes.

## Recommended Actions

- Increase customer footfall.
- Maintain inventory availability.
- Continue targeted marketing investment.
- Monitor regional performance differences.

## Risks and Limitations

Regression identifies association, not causation.

External factors not included in the dataset may also affect sales.

## Final Conclusion

The multiple regression model is recommended because it incorporates multiple business drivers and provides the most comprehensive view of sales performance.
