# Model Comparison

## Simple Regression 1

Dependent Variable: monthly_sales

Independent Variable: marketing_spend

R² = 0.167

Interpretation:

Marketing spend has a positive relationship with monthly sales but explains only 16.7% of variation.

---

## Simple Regression 2

Dependent Variable: monthly_sales

Independent Variable: footfall

R² = 0.736

Interpretation:

Footfall is strongly associated with monthly sales and explains 73.6% of variation.

---

## Multiple Regression

Dependent Variable: monthly_sales

Independent Variables:

- footfall
- marketing_spend
- inventory_availability_pct
- region_north

Interpretation:

The model combines operational and marketing drivers and provides a broader explanation of sales performance.

---

## Final Model Selection

Multiple Regression

Reason:

It combines multiple business drivers and provides more useful business insights than individual-variable models.
