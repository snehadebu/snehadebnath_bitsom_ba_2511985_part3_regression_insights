# Residual Analysis

Residual = Actual Sales - Predicted Sales

## Largest Positive Residuals

Examples:

- 97,911
- 92,220
- 63,470
- 19,690

Interpretation:

These stores performed significantly better than predicted. Additional factors not captured in the model may have influenced performance.

## Largest Negative Residuals

Several stores showed actual sales below predicted values.

Interpretation:

These stores underperformed relative to expectations and may have experienced local operational or demand challenges.

## Business Implications

Footfall explains a large portion of sales variation, but other variables likely contribute to performance differences across stores.
